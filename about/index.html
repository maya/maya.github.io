Hi, I’m Maya Benari.

I design scalable UI systems and infrastructure that help teams move faster, build with clarity, and reduce friction at scale.

Over the past decade, I’ve built and scaled design systems across government, healthcare, and enterprise—developing tools and platforms that support large, complex ecosystems. I helped launch the U.S. Web Design System, a foundational UI framework now powering hundreds of federal websites. Since then, I’ve applied that same systems thinking to enterprise-scale platforms.

Today, I focus on foundational UI systems, component architecture, design tokens, documentation, and the processes that enable teams to scale design with clarity and consistency—from foundations and components to the patterns they support. I also mentor designers and collaborate with cross-functional partners to align vision, reduce ambiguity, and strengthen systems practices across organizations.
