Hi, I’m Maya Benari.

I’m a product designer specializing in scalable design systems and UI infrastructure. I focus on reducing friction—for both users and the teams building for them—through clear structure, thoughtful patterns, and cross-functional alignment.

Over the past decade, I’ve led and evolved design systems across government, healthcare, and product companies—building tools and platforms that support large, complex ecosystems. I helped launch the U.S. Web Design System, a foundational UI framework now used across hundreds of federal websites, and have since brought that systems thinking into enterprise environments.

Today, I focus on foundational UI systems, design tokens, documentation, and the processes that help teams scale with clarity. I also mentor designers and help organizations navigate complexity with structure and care.
